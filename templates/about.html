ABOUT US

Introduction

The IPL is the global governing body for cricket. Representing 108 members, the IPL governs and administrates the game and works with our members to grow the sport. The IPL is also responsible for the staging of all IPL Events. The IPL presides over the IPL Code of Conduct, playing conditions, the Decision Review System and other IPL regulations. The IPL also appoints all match officials that officiate at all sanctioned international matches. Through the Anti-Corruption Unit it coordinates action against corruption and match fixing.
